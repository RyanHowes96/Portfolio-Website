About Me


Hello! I'm Ryan Howes.


Welcome to my portfolio of work. I'm a Computer Science graduate from Birmingham City University, where I achieved a 1st Class Bachelors degree with honours. This website will be regularly updated with various projects I have been working on recently, mostly involving user experience design. Graduating university and having worked as a systems developer has allowed me to explore different aspects of computer science and learn about new and interesting topics such as the web development and the user experience fields.

My duties as a systems developer involved the creation of systems intended to assist in improving the workflow of employees by allowing them access to an easy to use interface which receives and processes loan applications. The development environment Equinox was used as the backbone of all the in-house application used for this company, more information about Equinox can be found at the official website here: Equinox7.com

Currently, my passion lies with UX design. After learning about UI and UX design through one of modules at university I have started to expand my knowledge in the field through online tutorials and courses. This website, and all of my personal projects shown here are all developed through further learning in my own time. Alongside this I also partake in web development and design, where I continue to learn and develop HTML, CSS and Javascript programming skills.
